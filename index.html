Research & Projects

ML-Driven Discovery in Chiral Magnets

Investigating how grain boundary geometry affects Weyl points and DMI in B20 CoGe. I developed a Python pipeline to analyze ~13,000 interface structures using physics-aware SOAP descriptors. By leveraging UMAP and HDBSCAN for unsupervised clustering, I identified representative candidates for high-fidelity VASP DFT calculations.

Screening for Endotaxial Materials

NSF REU at University of Michigan: Endotaxial heterostructures utilize layer-by-layer switching in polytypic materials to create stable quantum states. I developed an elastic screening workflow to predict temperature-driven phase transitions in Transition Metal Dichalcogenides (TMDs), significantly reducing the computational cost compared to traditional phonon calculations.

Antimatter Detector Simulation

Optimizing detector placement is critical for capturing rare muon events. I utilize Monte Carlo simulations to predict the angular distribution of particles passing through magnetic fields. Using the Scikit-HEP ecosystem and ROOT, I analyze simulation data to maximize detection efficiency for our single-detector setup.
